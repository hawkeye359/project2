About

The CAR-147 (Basic) / B.Sc (Aeronautics) programe is a joint venture of Pawan Hans Limited (A Government of India Enterprises) and Jamia Milllia Islamia (Central University) set up through MOU signed on 27 July 2017 (alongwith MOU Addendum dated 14 June 2018), under Self Finance Scheme(SFS). The training orgnization has been established with an objective to provide a world class quality knowledge and practical training in aviation at par with latest technology enabling students competent for taking Modules Examination of CAR 66 requirements, in order to acquire DGCA License (AME license in Mechanical/Avionics stream). After successful completion of training syllabus, the students would be awarded a Certificate of Recognition as per CAR-147 (Basic) guidelines. Since the training syllabus has been framed in line with the syllabus prescribed by the University Grants Commission vide letter number 1-14/2016 (CP-II) dated 16 June 2017, the students would also be awarded B.Sc. (Aeronautics) degree by the Jamia Millia Islamia.
